1 - Context & Motivation

The visualization of global terrestrial temperature is a major topic of interest in the field of climatology and environmental science. With the increase in greenhouse gas emissions, climate change has become a critical issue for the future of our planet. Thus, understanding the evolution of the Earth's temperature is essential for making informed decisions about environmental policy.

In this context, data visualization is a valuable tool for exploring trends and patterns in climate data. Using the D3.js visualization library, interactive and dynamic visualizations can be achieved, providing an optimal user experience for exploring these complex data.
